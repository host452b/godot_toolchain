# Strategy And Management Prototype Questions

Use this reference to choose the smallest prototype for simulation, tycoon, or
systemic strategy concepts.

## Prototype Routes

| Risk | Best prototype | Example |
|---|---|---|
| Core verb and loop are unclear | Spreadsheet or paper first, Godot second. | Does build -> earn -> upgrade produce interesting choices? |
| UI drives the game | Clickable wireframe or simple Godot UI scene. | Can players understand state and next action in 3 minutes? |
| Feel depends on timing, agents, or map animation | Godot prototype. | Do customers, map spread, or travel ticks feel alive? |
| Economy may snowball or stall | Spreadsheet simulation plus scripted Godot counters. | Does income inflate after 20 minutes? |
| Opposing system may be too passive | Turn-based paper model or simple simulation. | Does cure/faction/opponent pressure force adaptation? |

## Sample Hypotheses

- Tycoon: "If players place facilities and see immediate customer use, they will
  want to reinvest; success means they make three different upgrade decisions
  without prompting."
- Historical sandbox: "If NPCs and factions move without player input, players
  will perceive the world as alive; success means they report a personal story,
  not only a completed task."
- Global simulation: "If the map visibly reacts to player tuning and opposition,
  players will feel strategic pressure; success means they can explain why they
  changed strategy."

## Cut List

Cut unless the prototype question requires it:
- Full save/load.
- Full art and animation set.
- Complete content list.
- Mod tools.
- All roles, countries, or facilities.
- Long tutorial.
- Production architecture.

## Observation Notes

Watch for:
- Does the player understand cause and effect?
- Do they know what to do next?
- Do they wait passively or make decisions?
- Do they discover a dominant strategy immediately?
- Do they ask for missing information that should be on the UI?
