# Systemic World And Atmosphere Examples

Use this reference when the "level" is a map, world dashboard, town network,
country graph, or management space instead of a traditional action level.

## World Forms

| Sample | World form | What must read clearly |
|---|---|---|
| Taiko Risshiden | Historical map with cities, factions, NPC locations, travel routes. | Where to go, who matters, what changed politically. |
| Kairosoft-style tycoon | Compact spatial layout with facilities, paths, staff, customers. | Throughput, congestion, value hotspots, expansion opportunities. |
| Plague Inc. | Global map with countries, transport routes, infection, cure response. | Spread, resistance, urgency, region differences. |

## Spatial / Map Design Questions

- What is the player's critical path through information?
- Which state changes must be visible at map scale?
- Which details belong in selected-object panels instead of the map?
- How does the map show opportunity, danger, and progress?
- Can the player identify the highest-priority region or facility without reading every number?

## Atmosphere By System

| System state | Visual cue | Audio cue | UX cue |
|---|---|---|---|
| Peaceful growth | Warm colors, steady agent movement, low alert density. | Light ambience, soft reward ticks. | Encourage planning and expansion. |
| Rising pressure | Pulsing alerts, highlighted bottlenecks, map spread. | Tension layer, warning motif. | Promote triage and prioritization. |
| Crisis | Strong contrast, urgent overlays, slowed or paused decisions. | Distinct alarm, ducked ambience. | Explain cause and offer response. |
| Recovery | Reduced alert density, stable rhythm, visible repair. | Calm return cue. | Reopen optional decisions. |

## Readability Checks

- Important silhouettes or overlays work at gameplay camera distance.
- Weather, post-processing, crowding, or particles do not hide hazards or goals.
- Color encodings have shape, icon, animation, text, or sound backup.
- The same map can support planning, diagnosis, and reward without changing meaning.
- Fast-forward does not turn the simulation into unreadable noise.

## Common Mistakes

- Treating a dashboard map as decoration instead of the core play surface.
- Showing every variable at once instead of layering information.
- Making the world feel static because NPCs, customers, or countries do not visibly react.
- Using atmosphere that hides the economic or strategic signal.
